The templates available as part of this stationery system have been updated to reflect the revised brand architecture for the University, as approved by University Senior Executive. If you have any questions about the brand architecture and the University’s strategic shift to masterbrand, email the brand team at brand-info@unimelb.edu.au

NOTE: Faculty logos are no longer AVAILABLE under the revised Brand Architecture.

Eligibility

Please refer to the University of Melbourne's Branding Policy (MPF1193) for details about staff and student eligibility for purchase of University of Melbourne branded stationery.

Approval

  • Clients are able to download a low-res, watermarked PDF of the purpose of proofing artwork.
  • No job will go into production unless submitted to print by the client.

Important: The client is ultimately responsible for final approval of all artwork submitted to print production via this system. Submitting to print is deemed as approval of artwork and authorisation to proceed with the order. Submitting to print is also deemed as confirmation that the client has followed any and all approval processes as required by their organisational unit and that the information presented on the stationery being ordered is in accordance with University of Melbourne's Branding Policy (MPF1193).

Design, Print & Merchandise (DPM) and the print supplier accept no responsibility for typographical errors, spelling mistakes or incorrect information on any item. No refunds or reprints are given after a final approved design has gone to print due to oversights by the client's proof reading. Reprints will be charged at full price.

Pricing

A price will be advised when you configure your order.

Turnaround and delivery

The stationery is printed once a week in order to obtain the best combination of economy and time. Orders approved by 1:00 PM Wednesday will be delivered to the UOM Mailroom on the following Monday afternoon, for delivery via Internal Mail (this usually takes 24-48 hours).

Stationery considerations

Please note that stationery items, particularly business cards can only hold a limited amount of information and should ideally contain essential contact information only. Business cards that require foreign language text to be added are not supported by the online system.

For formats that cannot be ordered online, please complete and submit the Offline Stationery Order Form.

Ordering Generic UOM Envelopes

Order a variety of sizes of envelopes pre-printed with the University crest logo using iProcurement in Themis. The University’s preferred supplier for office supplies is Complete Office Supplies (COS).

Ordering Personalised return address, BELIEVE envelopes and Australia Post reply paid envelopes

If you require a personalised return-to-sender address printed on the flap of your envelope, you will need to place a personalised envelope request via unimelb.printiq.com.
